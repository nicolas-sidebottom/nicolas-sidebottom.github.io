About Me

Just a small introduction to get to know me.

My name is Nicolas Sidebottom and I am currently a third year student at UCSD studying nanoengineering. Outside of my time as a student, I am a backwaiter working out of a restaraunt brewery in Mira Mesa called Gravity Heights. When I'm not working or studying, I enjoy spending time with my friends, training Jiu Jitsu and Muay Thai, reading, and working on personal projects. I also very much enjoy the outdoors, so if I'm not at home, you might find me in Alpine on a hike, or at one of the many beaches here in San Diego fishing for lunch. After recieving my B.S in nanoengineering, I hope to work full time as an engineer in either materials science or thermodynamic systems. In the far future (after I have retired) I would like to become a high school science teacher so that I can not only keep myself busy, but also give back to my community and help shape the next generation of scientists and engineers.
